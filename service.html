Devoted

I consider my greatest act of service to be the work that I do at Code Ninjas. At Code Ninjas, we teach young children how to code and build robots and circuits, but more importantly, we provide a safe and welcoming environment for our ninjas (students) to learn. We encourage our ninjas to collaborate and have fun, while we build the coders of tomorrow. Computer science is an ever-growing field, and we believe that kids cannot start learning the basics soon enough. Coding is the language of the twenty-first century, and our ninjas are building the foundation they need to use that language to build something truly great. I cannot wait to see the things that they will accomplish.
