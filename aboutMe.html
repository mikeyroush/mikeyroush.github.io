Adventurous

I am a computer engineering major at Texas A&M University. I aspire to use the skills I have learned in my degree to become the CIO of a company one day. In fifteen years, I hope to have several real estate investments that generate a passive income. But, beyond a successful career and monetary gain, I want to be a good son, father, mentor, and friend.


Oprah Winfrey once said, "Do what you have to do until you can do what you want to do, "and I fully buy into this mentality. However, I also believe a good work/ life balance is essential to a happy life. For that reason, I have several hobbies and interests. Some of these include the MS 150, mountain biking, football, waterskiing, traveling, and hiking.


Mountain biking, and biking in general, are quite possibly my favorite hobbies out of the list. I have gotten several bumps, scrapes, and bruises from mountain biking, but I cannot think of a better analogy for life. When you get knocked down, you have to get right back up. I enjoy biking even more when I am doing it for a cause. The MS 150 is a grueling 150-mile bike ride from Houston to Austin for charity. Whatever you do, do it to the best of your ability.
